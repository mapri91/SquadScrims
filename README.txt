# squadscrims
Discord bot for competitive matchmaking

// The idea behind this bot //

SquadScrims is your way into competitive matchmaking in Squad. Whether you want to play as a whole team or only with a few people, at SquadScrims you can find or create matches that fit your needs. With SquadScrims we want to make competitive gameplay in Squad more dynamic and centralized, so that you can play scrims more frequently, even if you donâ€™t have a full team on hand.

At SquadScrims you can create or join match lobbies and find other teams to play against. You can do further planning within the match lobby and communicate match details with the other teams. All of this can be done with just a few commands and the bot will do the rest for you.
